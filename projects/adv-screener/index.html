ADV Screener

A cleaner way to surface and compare investment adviser disclosures.

Started January 9, 2026 Updated March 9, 2026
AstroTypeScriptMDXSEC data

Problem

Adviser data is public, but the experience of navigating it is often fragmented and needlessly heavy.

Solution

Build a focused product surface that turns filing exploration into a fast, readable, filterable workflow.

Who it's for

Researchers, allocators, operators, and curious analysts who need a better way to interrogate adviser disclosures.

ADV Screener is meant to sit at the intersection of public data, better product thinking, and decision support.

Why it exists

Many diligence workflows begin with public filings, but the surrounding experience is rarely designed for modern research habits. The goal here is not only access, but legibility.

Key features

  • Filter filings by firm profile, disclosures, and structural signals.
  • Compare multiple advisers without getting lost in raw documents.
  • Surface change-oriented insights for ongoing diligence.

Roadmap

  • Launch a public overview page and initial dataset model.
  • Add smarter comparison views and saved research paths.
  • Explore a separate app deployment once the workflow stabilizes.
