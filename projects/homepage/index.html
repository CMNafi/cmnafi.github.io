Homepage

The premium personal front door for cmnafi.com, designed around writing, products, adventures, and work in progress.

Started March 13, 2026 Updated March 18, 2026
AstroTypeScriptCSSCodex

Problem

The old landing experience leaned too hard on a coffee concept instead of making it immediately obvious that this is C M Nafi's personal website.

Solution

Reframe the homepage around person-first navigation, editorial clarity, strong typography, and a lighter brewing metaphor reserved for work in progress.

Who it's for

Readers, collaborators, and first-time visitors who should understand within seconds that this site is a personal home for writing, projects, adventures, and current work.

The homepage is the front door for everything else on the site, so it needs to orient people quickly. It should feel personal, editorial, and calm before it feels thematic.

What changed

This version moves the site away from a coffee-led identity and toward a clearer personal structure. Writing, projects, adventures, and current work all have direct space on the page, while the brewing metaphor survives only as a light way to describe work in progress.

Why that matters

The goal is to make the site easier to understand without flattening its personality. A first-time visitor should be able to tell almost immediately that this is C M Nafi’s personal website, what kinds of work live here, and where to go next.

Key features

  • A personal hero anchored by name, descriptor, intro, and clear destination CTAs.
  • Direct sections for positioning, featured destinations, current work, selected work, and the scalable archive system.
  • Warm editorial styling with restrained motion and only subtle brewing accents where they actually help.

Roadmap

  • Keep refining destination copy and featured selections as the site grows.
  • Expand Adventures with more cricket records, travel logs, and story-led field notes.
  • Preserve a modular structure so new apps, notes, and public experiments are easy to add later.
