Homepage

The front door for cmnafi.com, redesigned as a modern coffeehouse studio with a brewing animation system.

Started March 13, 2026 Updated March 18, 2026
AstroTypeScriptCSSCodex

Problem

The old landing experience did not present the site as a clear, premium front door for writing, projects, and adventures.

Solution

Rebuild the homepage as a cohesive editorial experience with stronger hierarchy, clearer navigation, and a restrained brewing narrative that makes the site feel intentional.

Who it's for

Readers, collaborators, and anyone arriving at cmnafi.com who should immediately understand what is being built and where to go next.

The homepage is the current public front door for the site, so it needs to do more than look good. It has to orient visitors quickly, hold atmosphere without getting in the way, and reflect the level of care behind the writing and product work.

Why this version matters

This redesign moves the homepage away from a generic landing page feel and into a more editorial, studio-like experience. The brewing metaphor gives the page a clear internal rhythm, but the main job is still clarity: make the important destinations obvious, keep the motion subtle, and let the interface breathe.

What makes it current

The latest version focuses on warm typography, calmer surfaces, and a more deliberate animation loop that maps to four phases: intake, grind, brew, and serve. The result should feel alive without becoming noisy.

Key features

  • Two-column hero with a subtle staged coffee-brewing apparatus.
  • Clear paths into Blog, Projects, and Adventures without losing atmosphere.
  • Responsive, reduced-motion-friendly presentation across desktop and mobile.

Roadmap

  • Continue tightening copy and project spotlight selection as the site grows.
  • Add richer supporting visuals for sections that earn dedicated storytelling.
  • Keep the homepage aligned with the most current design language of the site.
