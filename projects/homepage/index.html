Homepage

A minimal front door for cmnafi.com that simply says Brewing and points people to the fuller work-in-progress page.

Started March 13, 2026 Updated March 18, 2026
AstroTypeScriptCSSCodex

Problem

The old landing experience leaned too hard on a coffee concept instead of making it immediately obvious that this is C M Nafi's personal website.

Solution

Keep the homepage extremely simple, center it around Brewing, and move the richer overview of writing, projects, adventures, and active work onto the brewing in progress page.

Who it's for

Readers, collaborators, and first-time visitors who should understand within seconds that this site is a personal home for writing, projects, adventures, and current work.

The homepage is the front door for everything else on the site, so it now does less on purpose. It should orient people quickly, feel calm, and avoid competing with the rest of the website.

What changed

This version keeps only the lightest brewing language on the front page. The fuller picture of writing, projects, adventures, and current work now lives on the brewing in progress page instead of crowding the landing view.

Why that matters

The goal is to make the site easier to understand without flattening its personality. A first-time visitor should be able to tell almost immediately that this is C M Nafi’s personal website, what kinds of work live here, and where to go next.

Key features

  • A minimal landing hero built around Brewing with only essential navigation cues.
  • A richer brewing in progress page that carries the fuller site overview and active work.
  • Warm editorial styling with restrained motion and only subtle brewing accents.

Roadmap

  • Keep refining destination copy and featured selections as the site grows.
  • Expand Adventures with more cricket records, travel logs, and story-led field notes.
  • Preserve a modular structure so new apps, notes, and public experiments are easy to add later.
