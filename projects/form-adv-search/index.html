Form ADV Search

A regulatory search tool for quickly navigating Form ADV disclosures and investment adviser filings.

Started December 31, 2025 Updated March 17, 2026
Google AI StudioSEC EDGARRegulatory data

Problem

Form ADV filings are public but navigating them through the SEC's default interface is slow, fragmented, and hard to search effectively.

Solution

A focused search interface that makes it easy to pull up adviser disclosures, scan key fields, and move between filings without getting lost in the raw SEC workflow.

Who it's for

Analysts, allocators, compliance teams, and anyone who regularly needs to review or cross-reference investment adviser registrations.

Form ADV Search is a live regulatory product built to make investment adviser filings more accessible.

Why it exists

Form ADV is one of the most important public regulatory documents in financial services — but the default experience for navigating it is built for compliance archivists, not researchers or analysts who need fast, targeted answers.

This tool sits between the raw SEC data and the end user, making it practical to search, read, and act on what’s in a filing without the usual friction.

What it does

The search interface lets you find registered investment advisers quickly, surface the fields that matter, and move between related filings without losing your place. It is designed to be fast and direct.

Key features

  • Quick search across registered investment advisers by name or identifier.
  • Surface key ADV fields without reading full PDF documents.
  • Fast navigation between related filings and adviser history.

Roadmap

  • Expand filtering by adviser type, AUM range, and disclosure flags.
  • Add side-by-side comparison for multiple advisers.
  • Export selected fields for downstream research workflows.
