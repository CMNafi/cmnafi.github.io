# Site Voice Guide

- Write in the first person unless there is a strong reason not to.
- Lead with lived detail, preference, memory, and opinion before explanation.
- If a sentence could describe any thoughtful builder on the internet, rewrite it.
- Use concrete nouns over abstractions. Prefer "books," "matches," "films," "Dinajpur," and "late-night note" over "signals," "surfaces," and "systems."
- Let taste show. It is fine to sound amused, skeptical, affectionate, or direct.
- Work pages should explain what friction I noticed, why it bothered me, and why I kept building.
- Cricket, books, and the journey chapters are the reference voice for the rest of the site.
- "Brewing" stays only where it still feels natural. Everywhere else, say the plain thing.
