About

A quick read on who I am, where I've been, and what drives me.

My story began in Bangladesh, the son of a lawyer and a teacher. I attended military school in Bangladesh for several years before setting off for Changshu, China to continue my education at an International Baccalaureate high school — and eventually made my way to Luther College in northeast Iowa. The world is, indeed, global.

As a data science and computer science double major with a love for the liberal arts, I understand the role technology plays in our lives and enjoy using data to tell a story. Through work with Luther College's admissions and institutional research teams, I applied research and data-science skills to global strategy and modernized historical datasets into interactive dashboards.

I've also taken those skills into the private sector — building an automated pricing system for a sustainable clothing resale store, doing data analytics at Dhakai.com (connecting sustainable manufacturers to global retailers), and co-founding Norsebooks.com, a peer-to-peer textbook marketplace for Luther College students that reached 70+ users and 300+ listed texts within two months of launch.

The amount of data being generated in the world is constantly growing. I'm here to help make sense of it — and build things that matter.
