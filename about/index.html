About

A digital home base for thoughtful work

This site is designed less like a portfolio and more like a public operating system — a place to gather writing, tools, experiments, and the ideas connecting them.

I’m interested in what happens when strong product taste meets deep curiosity: data tools that feel humane, writing that sharpens thinking, and systems that make public work easier to sustain.

Some things here are finished, some are early, and some are intentionally unfinished. That is part of the point. The site is built to hold work in motion.

Over time, this space will grow to include essays, project overviews, modular apps, notes, and live snapshots of what is currently brewing.
