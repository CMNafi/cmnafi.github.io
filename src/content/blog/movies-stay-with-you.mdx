Some movies last because of plot. Others last because they change the pressure in the room.

## Atmosphere matters more than explanation

A film can stay with me because it builds a world with enough internal weather that I can step back into it instantly.

## Timing is part of meaning

The right movie at the wrong time can pass by unnoticed. The same movie, seen months later, can feel uncannily direct.

## Certain scenes become coordinates

I don't always remember every beat. I remember a hallway, a face turning toward a window, a piece of music landing at exactly the right moment.
