A great book does not always announce itself right away. Sometimes it sits quietly for a few days and then keeps widening in my head.

## It changes my pace

The books I love most do not just entertain me or inform me. They change how I move through the next week. They make me read slower, notice more, or carry a sentence around for longer than is reasonable.
