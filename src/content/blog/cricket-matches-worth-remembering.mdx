The matches worth remembering are rarely just the biggest ones. They are the ones where the emotional geometry stays sharp years later.

## Pressure changes everything

You can feel when a match narrows into a contest of nerve. Every dot ball starts to carry more weight than a boundary from an hour earlier.

## Context deepens the memory

A chase means more when it sits inside rivalry, form, weather, and expectation.
