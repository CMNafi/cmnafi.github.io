ADV Screener exists because I got tired of the gap between public availability and actual usability. The filings are there. The patience they demand is the problem.

## Why it exists

I kept wanting a calmer first pass through adviser data: something that would let me compare, filter, and notice patterns without feeling like I was wrestling the interface before I had even started thinking.
