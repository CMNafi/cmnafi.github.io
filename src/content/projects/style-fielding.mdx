**An interactive tactical playbook built to demonstrate and transition between cricket’s major fielding templates and strategic variations.**

---

## The Concept: Strategic Morphs

Cricket is a game of constantly shifting geometry. As the match progresses from the aggressive powerplay overs into the restrictive mid-overs, and finally into the explosive death-overs, the captain must morph the field configuration.

**Fielding Style Playbook** provides a visual, interactive demonstration of these tactical phases. Guided by an elegant **Master Timeline Range Slider**, captains and players can drag the slider to watch all 11 player tokens glide smoothly across a gorgeous vector turf between four historic presets, showcasing the physical movement required for every major match variation.

---

## Tactical Presets Included

### 1. The Classic Slip Attack (Stage 1)
- **Phase**: Powerplay (Overs 1-6)
- **Strike**: Right-Handed Batter (RHB)
- **Tactical Intent**: In the opening overs, capturing early wickets is paramount. With only 2 fielders allowed outside the 30-yard circle, this ultra-aggressive layout places 0 outside, instead crowding the bat with 3 slips, a gully, and a silly point.

### 2. Balanced Mid-Overs Ring (Stage 2)
- **Phase**: Mid-Overs (Overs 7-15)
- **Strike**: RHB
- **Tactical Intent**: Once the powerplay restriction ends, up to 5 boundary riders are allowed. This standard blueprint drops 2 fielders deep (Deep Cover and Deep Mid-wicket) to guard major hitting zones, while the remaining 7 form a tight ring to deny singles.

### 3. The Left-Hander Trap (Stage 3)
- **Phase**: Mid-Overs (Overs 7-15)
- **Strike**: Left-Handed Batter (LHB)
- **Tactical Intent**: Demonstrates the mathematical mirror transition about the vertical pitch center. The off-side ring floats instantly to the opposite side of the turf, showcasing how a captain dynamically neutralizes left-handed strikes.

### 4. Death Overs Shield (Stage 4)
- **Phase**: Death Overs (Overs 16-20)
- **Strike**: RHB
- **Tactical Intent**: To prevent maximums and boundaries when batters are swinging with high lofted shots. Enforces the maximum 5 boundary riders (Deep Cover, Long-off, Long-on, Deep Mid-wicket, Deep Fine Leg) to establish a comprehensive defensive shield.
