The homepage is the front door for everything else on the site, so it needs to orient people quickly. It should feel personal, editorial, and calm before it feels thematic.

## What changed

This version moves the site away from a coffee-led identity and toward a clearer personal structure. Writing, projects, adventures, and current work all have direct space on the page, while the brewing metaphor survives only as a light way to describe work in progress.

## Why that matters

The goal is to make the site easier to understand without flattening its personality. A first-time visitor should be able to tell almost immediately that this is C M Nafi's personal website, what kinds of work live here, and where to go next.
