This project is less about shipping a homepage and more about getting a personal website to tell the truth.

## What changed

I stopped treating the site like a portfolio problem and started treating it like a voice problem. The challenge is not just where things go. It is whether the words sound like me once they get there.

## Why that matters

Books, cricket, work, films, and movement across places are all real parts of my life. If the site cannot hold those together without turning them into filler, then the design is not done yet.
