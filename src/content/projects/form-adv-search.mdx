This tool began with a very ordinary annoyance: I wanted answers from Form ADV faster than the normal workflow would allow.

## Why it exists

The SEC interface is not unusable, but it asks for too much patience. I wanted something that got me to the filing and the useful fields quickly, especially for repeat research.

## What it does

It keeps the job simple: find the adviser, surface the important context, and let the user keep moving instead of re-learning the same navigation every time.
