Manager Search sits right in the middle of the kind of work I keep wanting to improve: finance research that should feel clearer long before it feels clever.

## Why it matters

I do not need filings to look fancy. I need them to become usable faster. A name should resolve cleanly, the identifiers should be obvious, and the next question should come into focus without extra cleanup work.
