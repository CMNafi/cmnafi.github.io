TACTICAL MISSION ANALYSIS

During the initial 6 overs of a T20 match, powerplay restrictions allow a maximum of 2 fielders outside the 30-yard circle. In this aggressive configuration, the captain places 0 fielders outside, choosing instead to crowd the batter with 3 slips and a gully. This is designed to force edges from early movement of the new ball.
